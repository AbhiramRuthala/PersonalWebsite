(Jan 2024 - June 2024)

After a pretty rough sleeping patch in my junior year of high school and a buzz slowly cackling for neurotech, I found a beautiful intersection. By trying to experiment around with my sleep and tamper with neurotech concepts, I can gain a better understanding whilst working to improve my life. Here it is!

Stability - Mood Tracker: Leveraging Face Images to Improve Mental Health Outcomes.

(July 2024 - June 2025)

July 28th, 2024. 11:03 am.

I'm walking around in my room, my mind permutating endlessly. What if...? Why not...? Neurotech? Projects? It covered different aspects... until it landed on one beautiful thought.

Why can't your phone, that uses Face-ID, also read your mood as well?

That marked the start of almost a year-long project working to shift the frontier of mental health, leveraging AI, Swift, and photo analysis to improve mental health outcomes on electronic devices.

Check it out on the App Store!

Currently, we've got 95+ downloads across 12+ countries, and a consensus 5-star rating as well!

FoodTrack - Utilizing AI and photo analysis to allow people to make more informed choices with their consumption.

(July 2025 - Current. Backed by UVA Engineering)

One of the most fun moments with my friends also happened to be one where I realized something strange.

Our freedom on this trip was endless. We ate whatever we wanted to.

And we were 2 months before college—a genuine place of endless freedom.

So I wondered, from a set of infleunces, do we actually know what the food that we're eating is actually doing to us?

How are we so sure that the food we're eating is not harming us endlessly?

That slowly worked to birth FoodTrack, my way of envisioning a better life by allowing people to get information regarding their food to make better choices in their life.

Check out the app's Instagram page!

Currently, a prototype has been finalized, and marketing is well-underway as I yearn to learn more about the problem around me.
